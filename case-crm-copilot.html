Support CRM Copilot

Giving support agents an AI partner inside the tools they already live in.
Client: Customer support team Team: Agents, leads, product Stack: Existing CRM + AI copilot

The problem

Agents had to bounce between tickets, documentation, and past conversations to answer even routine questions. Response times were inconsistent and knowledge lived in veteran agents’ heads.

  • Slow ramp for new agents.
  • High cognitive load on complex tickets.
  • Context scattered across multiple screens.

The build

We embedded an AI copilot panel directly inside the CRM that understands tickets, history, and docs.

  • One-click summaries of long threads and customer history.
  • Suggested reply drafts that match tone and policy.
  • Inline search over help center and internal notes.

The impact

  • Faster, more consistent responses from the whole team.
  • New agents perform closer to experienced ones.
  • Leads spend less time firefighting and more time improving process.
