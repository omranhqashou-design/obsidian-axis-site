About

A small team focused on real systems, not demos.

We’ve sat inside real operations and know how painful fragile tooling can be. Obsidian Axis exists to modernize those systems, add AI where it creates leverage, and keep teams in control of their workflows instead of fighting their tools.

How we work

Simple, practical, and built around your operations.

No hype decks. We start from how work actually flows today and then design the smallest, highest-leverage changes that make every day easier for your team.

Step 1
Observe the real workflow
We sit close to the people using the tools — screenshares, floor walk-throughs, and reviewing the actual systems that run your business today.
Step 2
Redesign for clarity & speed
We identify the smallest set of changes that give you a big jump in reliability, visibility, and speed. AI is added where it truly reduces effort, not just to say it's there.
Step 3
Ship, test, then extend
We ship a working version, get feedback from real users, and then extend it in tight loops so the system evolves with your team instead of against it.

What Obsidian Axis can plug into your stack.
