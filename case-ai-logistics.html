AI-assisted Logistics

Using AI to surface the signal in thousands of shipment events and status changes.
Client: Regional logistics provider Team: Dispatch, ops, management Stack: Internal TMS + AI layer

The problem

Managers were drowning in status emails, notifications, and system updates. By the time a human saw an issue, the window to react was already closing.

  • Critical exceptions buried inside routine updates.
  • Ops team constantly reactive instead of proactive.
  • No clean daily summary of what actually mattered.

The build

We built an AI layer that watches shipment events and produces human-level summaries and alerts.

  • Auto-summarized exceptions for each lane and region.
  • Spotlight feed highlighting “things a manager should look at”.
  • Daily digest email that reads like a human report.

The impact

  • Managers catch issues earlier with less screen time.
  • Ops team focuses on decisions, not digging for context.
  • Better communication from dispatch to leadership.
