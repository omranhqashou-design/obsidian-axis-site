Smart Inventory Board

Turning a physical whiteboard into a live, shared source of truth for a dealership floor.
Client: Automotive dealer Team: Ops, sales, management Stack: Web app + internal systems

The problem

The sales floor was running off a physical whiteboard. Cars, salespeople, and deal status were tracked manually, constantly erased and re-written, and often out of date by mid-day.

  • No real-time visibility for managers.
  • Salespeople asking “who has what?” all day long.
  • Zero history — once erased, information was gone.

The build

We replaced the whiteboard with a digital inventory board projected on the showroom wall and accessible from any device.

  • Live board showing each vehicle, salesperson, and current status.
  • Quick filters for “available”, “in process”, “sold, not delivered”.
  • Simple admin controls so managers can update status in seconds.

The impact

  • Everyone on the floor sees the same live picture.
  • Less time hunting for cars, more time with customers.
  • Cleaner hand-offs between sales and management.
